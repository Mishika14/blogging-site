7-Tips for living a healthy lifestyle

Having a balanced diet is too much to ask for these days. In todays era , we have so much of less time to look upon our diet that we often ending up gaining lots of fat which is actually bad for our health . There are number of things which effects us when we gain lots of saturated and transfats.High blood pressure,Heart disease,Stroke,Metabolic syndrome,Fatty liver diseases and much more. 13% of adults in the world are obese. This is a high- time that we adapt a healthy diet and workout plan.
Being an Indian we cant really afford to have that perfect less-oily and less-carb diet. But this is not the drawback, we can still follow a healthy indian diet plan with all things by our side. We can do this by approaching 70/30 rule which says,excess fat loss is the major outcome of 70 % of diet and 30 % of diet.

Here are 7 tips for carrying out a healthy day

Morning drinks

Mornings can be started with healthy drinks such as water infused with chia seeds,luke warm water
lemon water with honey , green tea or any detox drink. This helps in cleansing body from inside and helps you
feel active.Remember that you should stay hydrated and drink 7-8 glasses of water daily.

Breakfast

To get better start for a day breakfast is very important, so one should have protien enriched diet such as Upma ,veg dalia
semia,idlis or dosa whole wheat sandwich, fruits etc.

Lunch

One must not skip lunch after breakfast, it is very important meal of the day.It is said that our body is most active at this time
so it is necessary to eat right.We can have rice,moong daal,kichdi,patty,bhindi ,chanda daal,pranthas with low oil,veg sabzi etc.

Snacks

You may not be hungry after a good lunch but here are some snack options, you can have roasted Makhana, poha,peanuts,protien
chaat and fruit salad.

Dinner

Early Dinner is benefical for weight loss and belly fat .Dinner should be light enriched with fibre as body takes 3-4 hours to process
food before sleep . One can consider having vez sabzi with roti,dalia kichdi with veggies,turai,whole wheat pasta.

Sweet Cravings

No one can stop sweet cravings so for this one can consider Energy ladoos/bars,jaggery,home-made cookies.

Exercise

It is one of the very important task which one should do everyday. Exercise can help in controling weight and staying fit also, it
improves mood ,boosts energy and promotoe better sleep. Exercise is possible in many ways like running,skipping,yoga,aerobic
zumba and based on your body capabilites. One must do 30min routine workout daily.
